Oil Cake

An oil cake ready to be devoured. This almighty dessert is usually enjoyed with an evening mate.

Easy to prepare, easy to bake, easy to eat.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 3 spoons of baking powder
  • A little spoon of vanilla essence
  • Bowl
  • Mixer

Steps

  1. Add both eggs to the bowl
  2. Put in it the sugar and the oil, and mix 'em
  3. Then add 1/3 of the milk with a cup of flower, and mix
  4. Repeat this process three times
  5. Add the vanilla essence
  6. Let it go straight into the pan and then into a 180-degrees-heated oven.
